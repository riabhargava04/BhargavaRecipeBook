No Oil Banana Bread

Ingredients:
2 bananas
1.5 cups of all purpose flour
2 teaspoons of cinammon
0.5 teaspoon of baking soda
0.5 teaspoon of baking powder
0.25 teaspoon of salt
0.5 cup of sugar
0.25 cup of greek yogurt
Top with your favorite toppings (walnuts and chocolate chips)

Steps:


1) Set the oven temperature to 350 degrees


2) Mix all the wet ingredients in a mixer (mix until combined)

Add in the eggs, sugar, greek yogurt, and mashed bananas


3) Mix all the dry ingredients in a mixer

Add in the all purpose flour, cinammon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt


4) Mix all the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients


5) Add in your favorite toppings


6) Transfer the batter into a pan and put into the oven for 50-60 minutes (use a toothpick to check if baked enough)
